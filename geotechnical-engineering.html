GEOTECHNICAL ENGINEERING

The geotechnical group provides engineering and on-site construction inspection services. Our Watsonville office houses a certified laboratory facility for soil, asphalt, and concrete testing. Our skilled engineers conduct design-level geotechnical investigations and provide comprehensive recommendations for challenging site conditions.
Our Services Include:
  • Geotechnical consulting
  • Investigations of potential building sites
  • Geotechnical and geologic hazard studies
  • Pavement design, forensic analysis, and consultation
  • Recommendations for shallow and deep foundation systems
  • Consultation for soil stabilization processes, products, and materials
  • Forensic analysis and consultation for failed and distressed projects
  • Recommendations for retaining wall and retaining system design
  • Slope stability analysis and design recommendations
  • Landslide remediation and repair recommendations
  • Coastal engineering investigations
  • Infiltration testing
  • Forensic engineering
