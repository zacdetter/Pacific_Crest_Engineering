WHO WE ARE

Engineers, inspectors & collaborators

WHAT WE DO

Pacific Crest Engineering Inc. (PCEI) is a full service geotechnical, chemical process and environmental engineering firm providing engineering services to both the public and private sectors of the Monterey Bay and Silicon Valley areas. We provide geotechnical and environmental consulting as well as materials testing of soil, asphalt and concrete. Founded in 2001, the firm principals have over 50 years of combined consulting experience within the local area. ​​
SMALL BUSINESS
Pacific Crest Engineering Inc. is a Certified Small Business Enterprise (#47199) with the State of California. We are listed in the California Unified Certification Program (CUCP) database under Geotechnical and Chemical Engineering Services, Geotechnical Testing Laboratory, Environmental Consulting and Sampling, and other Scientific and Technical Consulting Services.
EXCEPTIONAL QUALITY & SERVICE
Pacific Crest Engineering consistently showcases its proficiency in delivering prompt responses to milestone dates, coordinating field inspections, addressing requests from project team members, and meeting deliverables. We recognize that good communication serves as the cornerstone for maintaining a consistent approach in accomplishing project goals. We are committed to providing exceptional quality and service to our clients, most of whom have worked with our staff for many years.
