Roadway Improvement Projects

Pacific Crest Engineering provides geotechnical consulting, geotechnical investigations, pavement design recommendations and construction phase services for all types of roadway projects. Our staff has experience with city streets, county roads, highways, freeways, retaining structures, and other related roadway infrastructure. The Pacific Crest Team has worked with a variety of different agencies including the County of Santa Cruz, County of Monterey, Cal Trans, and numerous cities located within the Monterey Bay Area.
