WHO WE ARE

Engineers, inspectors & collaborators

WHAT WE DO

Pacific Crest Engineering Inc. (PCEI) is a full-service geotechnical, chemical process and environmental engineering firm providing services to both the public and private sectors of the Monterey Bay and Silicon Valley areas. We provide geotechnical and environmental consulting, special inspections, and materials testing of soil, asphalt and concrete. Founded in 2001, the principal engineers have over 50 years of combined consulting experience within the local area. ​​
SMALL BUSINESS
PCEI is a Certified Small Business Enterprise (#47199) with the State of California. We are listed in the California Unified Certification Program (CUCP) database under Geotechnical and Chemical Engineering Services, Geotechnical Testing Laboratory, Environmental Consulting and Sampling, and other Scientific and Technical Consulting Services.
EXCEPTIONAL QUALITY & SERVICE
PCEI consistently showcases its proficiency in delivering prompt responses to milestone dates, coordinating field inspections, addressing requests from project team members, and meeting deliverables. We recognize that good communication serves as the cornerstone for maintaining a consistent approach in accomplishing project goals. We are committed to providing exceptional quality and service to our clients, most of whom have worked with our staff for many years.
MEET

THE TEAM

Matt Maciel

Principal Geotechnical Engineer

Maciel has been involved in analysis, design, management, and construction observation of diverse geotechnical engineering projects, encompassing commercial and residential developments, bridges, landslide repairs. He possesses extensive expertise in geotechnical investigations, project management, and laboratory quality management. Maciel joined Pacific Crest in 2007, and became part owner in 2021.

Chris Johnson

Principal Civil Engineer

Since 2006, Johnson has worked on diverse geotechnical projects such as offices, residential developments, and government facilities, with expertise in construction techniques like soil mixing and retaining walls. He also manages and oversees construction projects. Johnson became part owner in 2021.

Jim Walker

Principal Chemical Engineer

Walker has provided comprehensive management, consultation, and design services for chemical, environmental, civil, and fire protection projects since 1989. His expertise spans process design, environmental permitting, and fire protection engineering for diverse industries, including higher education, semiconductor, biotechnology, pharmaceutical, and food processing.

Elizabeth Mitchell

Associate Geotechnical Engineer

For more than 30 years, Mitchell has provided management, consultation, and design services for diverse geotechnical engineering and material testing projects in Santa Cruz County. Her expertise includes complex karst conditions, coastal engineering, slope stability, and deep and shallow foundation systems, showcasing extensive experience in project execution and technical direction.

Soma Goresky

Associate Engineer

Goresky is a skilled civil and geotechnical engineer specializing in the San Francisco and Monterey Bay areas. She excels in supervising geotechnical investigations, providing recommendations for diverse structures, and managing complex design and construction projects since 1986.
